How the quiz works

Before you start the quiz, please take a moment to understand how the game works. This will ensure a smooth but still challenging experience for you.

  1. There are several flags shown around the world.
  2. The quiz has a limit of 3 minutes.
  3. There are no penalties for incorrect answers.
  4. You can't return to previous questions once answered.
  5. Final scores will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
  6. Enjoy the challenge!
